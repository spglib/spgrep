Calculate symmetrized irreps from given chain of solvable group.

Parameters

  • table (array, (order, order)) – Cayley table

  • factor_system (array, (order, order)) –

  • solvable_group_chain (list of single generator of coset) – Let G0 := G and G_{i} := G_{i-1} / < solvable_chain_generators[i] > (i = 0, 1, …). Then, G_{i} is normal subgroup of G_{i-1} and factor group G_{i-1}/G_{i} is Abelian.

  • atol (float) – Absolute tolerance to distinguish difference eigenvalues
